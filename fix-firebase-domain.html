Error: Firebase: Error (auth/requests-from-referer-https://cbrt-ui.test-are-blocked.)

Quick Fix: Use localhost

Immediate Solution: Access your app at http://localhost:5173
This should work immediately since localhost is pre-authorized.

Permanent Fix: Add Domain to Firebase

Step 1: Open Firebase Console

Step 2: Navigate to Authorized Domains

  • Click on the "Settings" tab
  • Scroll down to "Authorized domains"

Step 3: Add Your Domain

  • Click "Add domain"
  • Add: cbrt-ui.test
  • Also ensure these are added:
    • localhost
    • 127.0.0.1
    • cbrt-app-ui-dev.firebaseapp.com
    • cbrt-app-ui-dev.web.app
  • Click "Save"

Alternative: Update Your Hosts File

If you need to use a custom domain locally, you can also update your hosts file:

# Add to /etc/hosts (Mac/Linux) or C:\Windows\System32\drivers\etc\hosts (Windows) 127.0.0.1 cbrt-ui.test

Testing the Fix

After adding the domain:

  1. Clear your browser cache
  2. Restart your development server:
    npm run dev
  3. Try accessing the app again

  • cbrt-app-ui-dev.web.app
